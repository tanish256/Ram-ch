We Are Ram Champions

At Ram Champions, we’re more than just a coffee distribution company – we’re a community of passionate coffee lovers dedicated to quality, sustainability, and empowerment. Our partnership with CECOFA, a cooperative of over 13,500 farmers in Africa, fuels our mission to bring the best of African coffee to the U.S. market.

Our team thrives on collaboration, integrity, and a shared love for exceptional coffee. We pride ourselves on creating strong relationships with both our farmers and our customers, fostering a culture of transparency, respect, and mutual growth. With a deep commitment to environmental responsibility, gender and youth empowerment, and community development, we believe that every cup of coffee we deliver tells a story of care, connection, and positive impact. At Ram Champions, we’re not just about delivering great coffee – we’re about making a difference, one cup at a time.

Our Leadership Team

Our Leadership Team is a group of dedicated professionals with expertise in supply chain management, coffee quality assurance, and community-driven sustainability initiatives. Together, they ensure the seamless operation of our partnership with CECOFA, maintaining high standards in coffee production, distribution, and customer satisfaction. Each member is committed to advancing our mission of delivering premium, traceable coffee while fostering social and environmental development in coffee-growing communities.

Our Values

Authenticity

We are dedicated to maintaining transparency and trust at every stage of our coffee journey. From sourcing traceable coffee beans directly from CECOFA’s certified farmers to delivering premium coffee to our customers, we ensure authenticity in quality, process, and relationships.

Sustainability and Ethics

Sustainability and ethical practices are at the heart of everything we do. We actively promote environmental conservation through tree-planting, soil protection, and energy-efficient practices. Additionally, we empower communities by supporting gender equality, youth involvement, and social development in coffee-growing regions.
